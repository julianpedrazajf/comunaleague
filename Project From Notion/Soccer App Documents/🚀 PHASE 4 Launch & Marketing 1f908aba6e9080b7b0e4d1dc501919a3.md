# 🚀 PHASE 4: Launch & Marketing

**🧪 Beta Testing**

- Distribute via TestFlight (iOS) and Google Play Beta (Android)
- Pilot tournament in Cali to test usability and flow

**📣 Marketing Strategy**

- Partnerships with tournament organizers, venues, referees
- Social media campaigns: reels, stories, giveaways
- Referral program with discounts or rewards

**💰 Monetization**

- Player or team-based registration fees
- Local sponsorships and in-app ads (venues, gear, drinks)
- Premium features: custom badges, advanced stats, ad-free experience
